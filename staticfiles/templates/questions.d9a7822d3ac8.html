Survey Questions

  1. When did you complete this survey?
  2. On which date did you receive healthcare services?
  3. What is your full name?
  4. What is your unique patient identification number?
  5. In which city do you reside?
  6. What is your contact number?
  7. How old are you?
  8. Are you male, female, or prefer not to say?
  9. Which department of the hospital did you visit?
  10. How satisfied were you with the help desk services (Billing and admission procedures, health insurance, hospital policies, patient rights, etc.)?
  11. How would you rate the overall processes at the hospital?
  12. Were you satisfied with the waiting time at the outpatient department?
  13. How would you rate the waiting time for admission and discharge procedures?
  14. Were doctors readily available when needed?
  15. How punctual were the doctors in attending to your needs?
  16. How would you rate the overall quality of doctors?
  17. Were specialists readily available?
  18. How satisfied were you with the accuracy of your diagnosis?
  19. How would you rate the quality of the hospital's infrastructure?
  20. Were the medical equipment of high quality and readily available?
  21. How satisfied were you with the behavior of the general staff?
  22. Were the nursing staff responsive to your needs in a timely manner?
  23. How would you rate the overall efficiency of the nursing staff?
  24. How satisfied were you with the quality of diagnostic services?
  25. Were necessary drugs readily available?
  26. Were you introduced to any digital health tools during your visit?
  27. How satisfied were you with the care and guidance provided before and after the operation/treatment?
  28. How efficient was the hospital in handling unexpected emergencies and complications?
  29. How satisfied were you with the accessibility and accuracy of your medical information in the hospital's EMR system?
  30. Were you satisfied with the hospital's use of telemedicine or virtual health services?
  31. How would you rate the hygiene and cleanliness of the hospital?
  32. Were you satisfied with the quality and quantity of food provided?
  33. How would you rate the amenities provided for your family and relatives?
  34. How satisfied were you with your overall experience at the healthcare facility?
  35. Would you consider visiting the healthcare facility again?
